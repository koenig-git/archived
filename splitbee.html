My brother, Tobias Lins, started Splitbee, a friendly all-in-one analytics & conversion tool. It allows you to track visits on your websites, create funnels, run A/B tests, create email automations and more. All that while staying snappy and fun to use.

He did an amazing job with with the first releases, where it already was a great product. It got me interested to join in on the action, so I decided give it a complete facelift with a new identity and a fresh UI.

 
🐝
Checkout the Splitbee website
 
 

To kick things off I created a cute bee logo. Then the landing page followed, as well as the brand-new mobile-friendly dashboard.
